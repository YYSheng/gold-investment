About Us - Namaste

Namaste is a fast-growing precious metal brand launched by ubest gold, an East Asian subsidiary of gain capital, for the East Asian market. At the same time, as an important layout of gain capital in the field of precious metal investment in East Asia, the group is a global online trading service provider (New York Stock Exchange code: GCAP). As a leader in the rapidly developing online precious metal trading profession, gain capital provides retail customers and institutional investors from more than 140 countries around the world with services and skills products such as execution, clearing and protection of online precious metal and contract for difference (CFD) transactions.

Business Model

Our business model is F2C. That is Customer to Factory. Our company delivers the products designed by customers directly to the factory OEM through the terminal. Customers can place an order through Namaste Gold software. There are two payment modes, full payment and prepayment, which can be chosen by the customer.
